## ‚ôªÔ∏è Tutorial: How to Estimate Environmental Footprints


### ‚ö° 1. Energy Consumption

**Formula:**

$$
\text{Energy Consumption (Wh)} = \text{PUE} \times t \times \left( n_{c} \times P_{c} \times u_{c} + n_{m} \times P_{m} + n_{s} \times P_{s} \right)
$$

**Components:**
- üß† CPU/GPU: Thermal Design Power (TDP), core usage
- üß† Memory: Allocated GB √ó 0.3725 W/GB
- üíæ Storage: Active GB √ó 0.006 W/GB
- üè¢ Infrastructure: Adjusted using PUE (avg ~1.5)

**Alternative (if time series available):**

$$
\text{EnergyConsumption (Wh)} = \text{PUE} \times \sum_{i=0}^{t} \left( n_{ci} \times P_{ci} \times u_{ci} + n_{mi} \times P_{mi} + n_{si} \times P_{si} \right)
$$

---

### üåç 2. Carbon Footprint

**Definition:**  
Greenhouse gas impact in kg CO‚ÇÇe

**Main Formula:**

$$
C = E \times CI
$$

Where:
- $C$: Carbon emissions (kg CO‚ÇÇe)  
- $E$: Energy consumed (kWh)  
- $CI$: Carbon intensity (kg CO‚ÇÇe/kWh)

**Alternative (if CUE available):**

$$
C = E_{IT} \times CUE
$$

Where:
- $CUE$: Carbon Usage Effectiveness

---

### üíß 3. Water Footprint

**Scopes:**
- Scope 1: On-site cooling
- Scope 2: Electricity generation
- Scope 3: Hardware manufacturing (embodied)

**Operational Water Footprint:**
$$
\text{Water}_{\text{Operational}} = \sum_{t=1}^{T} e_t \cdot \left( \rho_{s1,t} + \theta_t \cdot \rho_{s2,t} \right)
$$

Where:
- $\rho_{s1,t}$: On-site WUE
- $\rho_{s2,t}$: Off-site WUE
- $\theta_{t}$: Power Usage Effectiveness
- $e_t$: Energy consumption at time $t$

**Embodied Water Footprint:**

$$
\text{Water}_{\text{Embodied}} = \frac{T √ó W}{T_0}
$$

**Simplified Total Water Estimate:**

$$
W = E \times WUE
$$

Typical WUE: ~1.8 L/kWh  
Note: Embodied water often excluded due to data limitations
